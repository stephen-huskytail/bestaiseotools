# Content Quality Bar

Every page published on BestAISEOTools.com and LocalSEOAutomation.com must meet these minimum standards. This bar exists because short, unsourced pages do not rank and do not convert.

## Word Counts

| Content Type | Minimum Words |
|-------------|---------------|
| Blog post   | 1,800         |
| Review      | 2,500         |
| Comparison  | 3,000         |

These are floors, not targets. Complex topics often require more depth.

## Research & Evidence Requirements

Every page must include:

- **2+ expert or vendor quotes** — attributed with name, title, and source. Pull from vendor docs, interviews, official blog posts, or verified expert commentary. Never fabricate.
- **3+ third-party citations** — authoritative external links that are NOT competitor affiliate sites. Acceptable sources listed below.
- **1+ original data point or unique opinion** — something readers cannot find elsewhere. This could be: original testing results, survey data, pricing analysis, workflow screenshots, or a contrarian take backed by reasoning.

### Approved Citation Sources

| Source Type | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| Industry analysts | Gartner, Forrester, IDC, G2 Research |
| Vendor documentation | Official docs, changelogs, pricing pages, help centers |
| Academic / research | Peer-reviewed papers, university research, .edu sources |
| Reputable industry blogs | Search Engine Journal, Moz, Ahrefs Blog, HubSpot Blog |
| News outlets | TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, major business publications |

**NOT acceptable**: Other affiliate/review sites, content farms, unattributed social media posts, forums without verification.

## On-Page SEO Checklist

Every page must include the target keyword in:

- [ ] Title tag
- [ ] H1 heading
- [ ] URL slug
- [ ] First paragraph (within first 100 words)
- [ ] Meta description

### Structure Requirements

- [ ] H2/H3 hierarchy that follows logical content flow — no skipping levels
- [ ] 3+ internal links to related pages on the same site
- [ ] FAQ section with at least 3 questions (triggers FAQPage schema)

## E-E-A-T Requirements

### Author Attribution

Every page needs:

- Author name (real person, not brand)
- Author bio with relevant credentials or experience
- Link to author archive page

### Freshness Signals

- Last-updated date visible on the page
- Review/update schedule documented internally

### Transparency

- **Methodology box** for reviews/comparisons explaining how we test
- **Affiliate disclosure** on every page with affiliate links (required by FTC)
- **Correction policy** for fixing factual errors

## Voice & Style

Write like someone who actually uses these tools:

- **First-person experience** where you've tested the tool yourself
- **Concrete examples** over vague claims ("I exported 500 keywords in 2 minutes" beats "fast export")
- **Screenshots and visuals** showing real workflows, not stock photos
- **Honest limitations** — every tool has tradeoffs, name them
- **Active voice** — "Surfer SEO analyzes your content" not "content is analyzed by Surfer SEO"

Avoid:

- Generic filler ("In today's digital landscape...")
- Unsubstantiated superlatives ("the best tool ever")
- Keyword stuffing
- Walls of text without visual breaks

## Pre-Publish Checklist

Before any page goes live:

1. [ ] Word count meets minimum for content type
2. [ ] 2+ expert/vendor quotes with attribution
3. [ ] 3+ third-party citations from approved sources
4. [ ] 1+ original data point or unique opinion
5. [ ] Target keyword in title, H1, URL, first paragraph, meta description
6. [ ] H2/H3 structure is logical
7. [ ] 3+ internal links added
8. [ ] FAQ section with FAQPage schema
9. [ ] Author bio with credentials
10. [ ] Last-updated date displayed
11. [ ] Methodology/disclosure box present
12. [ ] Affiliate disclosure visible
13. [ ] Page passes Lighthouse accessibility audit (score 90+)
14. [ ] Featured image is original or properly licensed

## Enforcement

Pages that do not meet this bar will be:

1. Flagged in the weekly audit
2. Prioritized for rewrite based on traffic potential
3. Noindexed if they cannot be brought up to standard within 30 days
